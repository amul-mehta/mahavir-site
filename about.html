Founded in the year 1990 by Mr. Sunil R Mehta and Mr. Rajesh P Devpura, Mahavir Foundry has come a long way in Manufacturing C I Castings for the Pump, Industrial Valves, General Machinery, Pneumatics Industry. Our product range covers all areas of Industry where casting is being used. With over two decades of experience backing the company, It has consistantly exceeded expectations of clients in terms of quality and services of the products manufactured. And we are constantly strive hard to exceede the standards. This perseverence has led Mahavir Foundry to be an ISO 9001:2008 Certified company.

The Company has also achieved a repute in domestic market and caters to leading manufactures in Pneumatic Controls sector, Oil & Gas Sector, Chemical Sector, Construction sector etc. Our organization is counted amongst the distinguished manufacturers and suppliers of Graded C I Castings, SGI (Ductile Iron), All type of Steel Casting in the weight ranges of 1 kg to 1000 kg.

Our manufacturing plant covers 16,000 square feet, in Odhav GIDC Ahmedanad, which contains our State of the art Induction Furnace (Inductotherm), Sand Molding, Core Making,Casting Grinding & Cleaning(Shot Blasting), and Inspection Departments. We also have In-House Machining facilities.

We also house a newly incorporated Shell Moulding Machine and Shell Core Shooter Machine having PLC Control. The Shell Moulding Machine is having a pattern plate size of 450mm X 600mm. The Shell Core Shooter Machine has a plate size of 300mm X 375mm. The machine can mould upto 50 mould boxes per hour. Machine Moulding enables us to manufacture Castings in bulk quantities without compromising on the smooth surface finish and weight of the castings.

Our foundry contains a 1000 Kg and a 500 kg capacity electric melting units. These electric furnaces provide a wide variety of molten Iron alloys for our C.I, S.G.I, M.S & S.S casting process. The certified Iron alloys include: IS-210, FG-220 to FG-260, ISO 1083 1987 (SGI) (International), WCB, WC6, CF-8, CF-8M Grades.

Quality reproduced with a strict adherence to the customer's requirements are the hallmarks of our cast components – which can be processed ready for assembly if necessary.Consistent melting practices and constant chemical and physical properties testing of the metal assures that all metal poured at our end meets or exceeds industry standards.

We have managed to widespread our manufacturing activity successfully on the highest level with the help of our expert professionals. Our team has proved to be the backbone of the organization. The inspection tools and trained technicians are there to ensure strict quality control process. They helps in checking the quality right from the raw material inspection to the dispatch of finished goods. Moreover, they work in close coordination with each other to attain the organization's goal.

We have already received an ISO 9001-2008 certification, which helps in gaining the a reputed position in the global market. Our main aim is to meet the customer expectations on quality products. For this purpose, we have established a well equipped laboratory for the quality maintenance as per customer's requirements.

Some of the equipments on which the products are being tested:

  • Brinell Hardness Testing Machine
  • Spectrometer
  • Inverted Metallurgical Micro-Structure with Image Analyzer
  • Immersion Temperature Pyrometer
  • Carbon-Silicon Analyzer
